Aside from forging weapons and armor, we offer world-class, one-on-one instruction in different styles of fencing. If you are more interested in classic, academic, sport-style fencing, we offer training in the epee, sabre and foil! If you would like to take it to the next level and learn the proper historic stances, maneuvres, tactics and fighting styles, we also offer instruction in weapons such as the timeless Longsword, the deadly Foil & Dagger combination, as well as Archery!
